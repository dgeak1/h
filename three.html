01. Generational Mastery

Insects use Holometabolism (complete metamorphosis) to ensure the survival of the species. By having larvae and adults occupy different niches, they eliminate generational competition.

Genetic Redundancy

High offspring counts allow for rapid evolutionary filtering against environmental changes.

Information Transfer

Pollination API

Waste Management

Foundation Species
