Entomology: Life & Generations

Exploring the complex social architecture of the insect world.

The Life Cycle

1
Egg: The genetic blueprint of the colony.
2
Larva: The growth phase; focused entirely on eating.
3
Pupa: The biological "re-coding" into adult form.
4
Adult: Specialization into workers, soldiers, or royalty.

Social Hierarchy

The Queen: The reproductive heart of the family.

Workers: The infrastructure and maintenance crew.

Soldiers: Specialized defense mechanisms.

Drones: Genetic couriers for future generations.

The Collective Intelligence

Unlike humans who act as individuals, social insects function as a Superorganism. Every action—from cooling the hive to building bridges—is done through decentralized "Pheromone Logic." No one is in charge, yet everything gets done perfectly.
