Minutes of the Emergency Faculty Executive Board Meeting

Date: March 21, 2026 Time: 02:05 PM — 03:03 PM

1. Call to Order & Opening Remarks

The meeting commenced at 02:05 PM. The Co-Staff Adviser, Mr. Fasoro, opened the floor by stating the primary objective of the gathering: to address internal frictions and procedural irregularities currently affecting the Board’s operations.

2. Attendance

Members Present:

  • Mr. Fasoro (Co-Staff Adviser)
  • The President
  • The Vice President
  • The Speaker
  • General Secretary
  • Assistant General Secretary
  • Financial Secretary
  • Public Relations Officer (PRO)
  • Welfare Director
  • Social Director
  • Sports Director
  • Treasurer

3. Review of the "Fresher Night" Proposal

A significant portion of the meeting focused on the Social Director's planning of the upcoming Fresher Night. Concerns were raised regarding the invitation of "DJ Chicken." The following points were noted:

  • Impact Assessment: The Board expressed that the choice of guest is considered "low impact" for the student body and does not align with the academic or social standards of the faculty.
  • Financial Accessibility: Concerns were raised that the proposed ticket pricing would create "classism," making the event inaccessible to a large portion of the student population.
  • Defense: The Social Director argued that he has logistical plans in place to support his vision; however, the Board found these justifications insufficient.

4. Procedural Irregularities & Award Voting

It was revealed that an awards voting link had been circulated and students had begun making payments without the knowledge or authorization of the Den, The Staff Adviser, the President, or the executive body.

The Staff Adviser expressed deep concern over this lack of transparency. It was clarified that no event or sub-program (like awards) can be publicized or monetized until it has been vetted and approved by the President and the broader executive body.

5. Conduct, Character, and Hierarchy

The Staff Adviser addressed a recent telephone conversation with the Social Director, describing the Director's tone as unprofessional, rude, and insubordinate. He emphasized that:

  • Good character and respect are non-negotiable values for any executive member.
  • Hooliganism or chaos should never replace calm, diplomatic problem-solving.
  • Official roles are distinct: The President must provide consensus, the General Secretary handles official correspondence (as noted by the recent cancellation letter), and the Staff Adviser through the Dean must provide final validation.

6. Clarification of Executive Roles

The Co - Staff Adviser briefly outlined the standard operating procedure for the board:

  • The President: Acts as the primary signatory and the "ground" for all agreements.
  • General Secretary: Responsible for formal documentation and communication (the "letter-writer" of the board).
  • Social/Sport Directors: Creative leads who must still submit all plans for collective executive agreement before moving to the Adviser and Dean for final approval.

It was noted that the Sports Director had previously made similar procedural errors but had since acknowledged the mistake and appealed for peace.

7. Resolutions & Rebuttals

  • Social Director's Response: He formally pleaded that no disrespect was intended toward the Staff Adviser, claiming his frustrations during the call were directed toward the President and he is requesting for an apology from the President for the embarrassment the action might have caused.
  • President's Stance: The President confirmed he was unaware of the voting link or the full details of the process, validating the claim of a breakdown in communication between the executive members. Which was addressed as it is necessary and vital for progress
  • Speaker's Verdict: The Speaker emphasized that "nothing is to be done in isolation." He stated that without hierarchy and transparency, no event can hold. And pleaded on behalf of the Social Director that the Staff Adviser let the calling sitution or anything that may have transpired be looked over so things can move on smoothly.
  • Welfare Contribution: He stated that transparencty above all is very important and that the body needs to work as one to make progress and truly this is keen for the betterment of all.
  • Financial Secretary: She emphasized the importance of accountability and transparency in all board activities and that things should go procedurally between members. Without criticizing either parties, she pleaded that the issue be a thing of the past and all parties involved should please not be offended and let everything go on smoothly.

8. Adjournment

The Financial Secretary prayed for peace and unity among all members. The Staff Adviser concluded by demanding a formal letter of apology from the Social Director for his conduct. The meeting was adjourned at 03:03 PM.
